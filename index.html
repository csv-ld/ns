A static site for serving vocabularies, organized by year-month date of publication and by organization/repo namespace (ns).

Path format: 

/YYYY/MM/org/vocab#

Examples: /2021/05/csv-ld/core#, /2021/05/csv-ld/demo#.

Each vocabulary URI resolves to a 

text/html
response with a
in its 
head
that provides the vocabulary in an RDF format, e.g. 
text/turtle
. This method of linking follows the example of RSS feeds.
